Friends

Parties, get-togethers and hangouts

Whenever I go and do something with my friends, whether it's visiting a festival, going on a city trip or just hanging out, I love taking a camera with some film and making some shots. This is a selection of the pictures I liked best.

I have an ever expanding collection of cameras on which I've shot these photos. These currently include:

  • Canon AF35M (Autoboy)
  • Olympus XA
  • Olympus OM-2
  • Zeiss Ikon Contessamatic E
  • Minolta XD7
  • Nikon F-801
Most pictures were shot on either Kodak Tri-X or Ilford HP5+, though lately I've really been loving Adox Silvermax.
