Italy

Florence and Bologna with friends

I have a group of friends with which we try to go on a (short) vacation once a year. This year marked the third edition, in which we rented a large (but fairly shoddy) villa in the hills of Tuscany, between Bologna and Florance. This album is mostly holiday snapshots and portraits of these friends.

All shots were taken with my Minolta XD7. The black-and-white images were shot with a Rokkor MD 50mm f1.4 lens and shot mostly on Adox Silvermax. These were also developed in the corresponding Silvermax developer for 11 minutes. The color photos were shot on Fuji Velvia 100 using a Rokkor 28mm f2.8 lens, and developed by my local lab.
