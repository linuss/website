Street photography

Inspired by the likes of Garry Winogrand, Fan Ho and Henri Cartier-Bresson, I sometimes go out and candidly take photos of strangers.

If you are one of these strangers and would rather not have your picture on my site, please let me know and I'll take the image down as soon as possible!
