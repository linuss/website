Amsterdam Zoo

This album contains images I shot while visiting the Amsterdam Zoo, Artis, on a super sunny day last June. All images are shot on my Minolta XD7 on Kodak Tri-X 400, using a Minolta MD 135mm 3.5 lens. I develop my own film, this time using Kodak HC-110 for 5 minutes.
